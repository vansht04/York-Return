## 🌌 The Concept
**YorkReturn** is a modern, secure, and interactive digital directory built for the York University community. It replaces scattered group chats and traditional paper flyers with a single, real-time, geolocated platform to help students recover their lost belongings safely[cite: 1].

---

## ✨ Core Features
* **📍 Interactive Map:** Geotagged pins powered by Leaflet to visualize where items were lost or found[cite: 1].
* **🔍 Advanced Search:** Filter by category, status (Active vs. Resolved), and item type[cite: 1].
* **📝 Quick Reporting:** Integrated modal to flag coordinates, upload details, and specify handover preferences[cite: 1].
* **🛡️ Safe Handover:** Built-in guidance for direct meet-ups or drop-offs at campus safety zones[cite: 1].
* **💬 Community Pulse:** Dedicated testimonial portal and live feedback widget for a better campus experience[cite: 1].
